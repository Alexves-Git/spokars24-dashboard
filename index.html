⚠️ Fictional Exercise Scenario

This dashboard and all associated data are entirely fictional. They have been created for the SMH CrypticChallenge (C-CHA), a synthetic modeling exercise designed to train teams in epidemic situational awareness, data interpretation, and forecasting under realistic but simulated conditions.

The scenario involves a novel paramyxovirus designated Spooky-like Virus 2024 (SPOK-24), causing Spooky-Associated Respiratory Syndrome 2024 (SPOKARS-24). The pathogen, epidemiological data, WHO briefs, news articles, and all surveillance outputs referenced in this exercise are fictitious and do not represent any real public health event.

The global spreading simulation is generated using the GLEAM (Global Epidemic and Mobility Model) framework, with 3,253 subpopulations across 232 countries. US state-level data is aggregated from 483 basin-level subpopulations. A detailed ABM model (EpiHiper) has been used in California, Virginia, France and UK and initialized with importations provided by the global model.
